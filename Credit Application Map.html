How this process works
When a credit app is submitted, DocuWare first checks if it's a cash deal (goes straight to admin review) or a financed deal (runs through up to 4 lender attempts). Each lender attempt follows the same pattern: the app is sent to the lender → 2-hour automated wait → DocuWare checks for a result. If no result comes back automatically, the admin manually checks. From there, the admin reviews and the outcome is either Approved, Declined (moves to next lender), or a final decision is needed.
